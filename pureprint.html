Four months of input forms

UI Designer

Pureprint Group,
Uckfield, U.K

Team —
Back-end - Ali Shah,
Colin Wilkinson ...

Pureprint Group is one of the largest commercial printers in the country. I was taken on as a Junior Developer but in time also took on the role of Designer. The majority of the projects that I was involved in were to design and develop MVC (Model, View, Controler) forms for active and potential clients to estimate the cost of a project and guide them in sending their data to the print team.

Their current forms and web apps were outdated in their design and they were looking for me to re-design and develop new content to an MVP (Minimum Viable Product), which would reflect the current trends in design.

The timeframe for each project was limited. Because of this, I used Bootstrap for layout and structure, which allowed me to give more attention to the design.

Although I was also not able to work alongside a copywriter or UX designer to form consistent language and information architecture across the projects, I learnt an incredible amount in such a short time. This was largely due to a fantastic mentor, Ali Shah, and also being given 100% responsibility for the design and front-end development of each project.
