This project was a re-design of the user interface used by HSBC call centre agents. The timeframe was 6 - 12 months.

I joined the project six months in and the information architecture and preliminary designs were already signed off. The product had been divided into three key components: the carousel; the work station; and a footer with the agent’s ‘call to actions.’

I worked closely with the UX team and the lead UI designer but was also given the opportunity to deviate from the on-going developments and work upon an alternative solution.

By the end of the project eighteen screens of information had been condensed down into four ‘carousels,’ which surfaced the information the agents needed at the time they needed it.
